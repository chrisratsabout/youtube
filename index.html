YouTube

Chris’s work on YouTube is with the Husky Raid channel, centered around the popular video game Destiny. He provides the score for their machinima series “Destiny Cops” as well as song recreations for their parodies.


Below are a few videos featuring his work. For more videos, go to the Husky Raid YouTube channel and subscribe!

Dreadnaught

His first effort with the group was the popular “Dreadnaught” rap in 2015. He provided the backing track which was a custom recreation of Macklemore’s “Downtown”.

Destiny Cops

Chris’s music began to be featured at the start of Destiny Cops season 2. His score is an eclectic mix of styles ranging from orchestral, synths, metal, etc. The videos below are the Destiny Cops musical episode and the song “Training Day” from the original soundtrack.



Parody Songs

The Husky Raid channel creates Destiny parody songs usually around Christmas time. Chris contributed the backing tracks for the songs below. These are recreations of Mariah Carey’s “All I Want For Christmas Is You” and Eartha Kitt’s “Santa Baby”.
